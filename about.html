About Us Page

Lee and Tee is a family business started in 2020 with the idea to bridge the financial gap due to Covid challenges. We have successfully thrived inspite of the difficult times and have seen a growth in the fashion styles that we offer. Our styles are carefully selected from World's top designers.

Quality is something that best describes us. We sell 100% authentic designs and material hence our customers get the best value for their money.
